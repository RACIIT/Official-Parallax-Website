PARALLAX
UI/UX Hackathon

Project PARALLAX is a hackathon introduced by the Rotaract club of IIT from the year 2020. Its main objective is to provide an opportunity for the gifted undergraduates of Sri Lanka to showcase their talents in designing and coding websites of high quality in a competition.

Parallax aims to provide the guiding light for gifted designers to build their personalities and brand as creative developers and designers. This will bring out the hidden talents of the participants so that they can polish and groom themselves well to face the competitive world of developers.

See more on our official RACIIT website
